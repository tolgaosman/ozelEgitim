Our Mission

A Community Where Every Mind Fits

Bloom Academy was established with a singular vision: to break the barriers of traditional education and build an inclusive learning sanctuary. We welcome kids with Autism Spectrum Conditions (ASC), ADHD, Dyslexia, Down Syndrome, and various learning styles.

✓
Therapy-Integrated Classrooms

Therapy targets are woven directly into daily classroom assignments so students learn naturally.

✓
Neuro-Inclusive Design

Low-glare lighting, soundproofing, visual schedules, and alternative seating options in every room.

For Families

The Parents' Navigation Guide

Navigating special education can feel overwhelming. We are here to simplify the process step-by-step.

1

Initial Assessment Chat

We host a relaxed, informal meeting with you and your child to observe their interests, communication patterns, and sensory comfort zones.

2

Collaborative IEP Draft

Our educators, speech therapists, and occupational therapists construct a custom goals roadmap, which is iterated directly with you.

3

Sensory & Setup Alignment

We customize the classroom furniture, sensory supports, and tactile materials specifically matching your child's sensory plan.

4

Continuous Progress Tracking

Weekly summaries, video updates, and regular collaborative meetings keep you connected to every breakthrough and success.

Visual & Color Psychology

Designed intentionally for neurodiverse individuals. Standard primary colors can over-stimulate. We utilizes soft, calming organic tones with excellent contrast scores (WCAG AA 4.5:1 ratio).

Accessible Typography

We leverage Lexend, a font specifically engineered by researchers to improve reading speeds, fluency, and text comprehension for individuals with dyslexia, paired with Inter for clean, minimalist layouts.

03. Interactive Accessibility Showcase

Unlike traditional layouts, Bloom Academy incorporates a built-in accessibility framework offering users full cognitive control over their reading environment.

A

Dyslexia Adaptation Options

Toggles layout into the research-proven Lexend typeface, expanding letter spacing and word bounding boxes to prevent characters from blurring together.

B

Focused Reading Guides

A reading ruler guide tracks cursor movements, masking secondary paragraphs to reduce distractions and help children stay on the active text line.

C

Dynamic Audio Assistance

Integrates speech synthesis that reads aloud hovering sections, helping pupils with speech therapy or reading barriers digest text independently.
